Message from the Commanding Officer

Welcome to our National Cadet Corps unit. As the Commanding Officer, I am proud to lead this exceptional group of young cadets who represent the future of our nation. The NCC stands for discipline, leadership, and patriotism, values that we strive to instill in each cadet.

Our training program is designed to develop character, comradeship, discipline, leadership, secular outlook, spirit of adventure, and the ideals of selfless service among the youth of our country. We are committed to providing experiences that will help shape our cadets into responsible citizens and future leaders.

I encourage all cadets to embrace the opportunities provided by the NCC and to live by our motto of "Unity and Discipline" (Ekta aur Anushasan).

Col. Rajesh Kumar
Commanding Officer, NCC Unit
