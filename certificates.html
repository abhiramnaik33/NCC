NCC Certification Programme

Certification Programme

The eligibility conditions and the procedures for the Certificate Examination for Cadets of Senior and Junior Division/Wing NCC (All wings) are as follows:

  • Must be in the second year of NCC.
  • Must have attended an Annual Training Camp.
  • Must have attended at least 75% of total training periods.
  • Break in service must not exceed 12 months.
  • If exceeded:

    "On rolls for 2 years and attended 75% training, must attend additional 45 periods."

  • Must be in the second year of SD/SW NCC training.
  • Must have attended an Annual Training Camp/NIC/Attachment Training.
  • 10 bonus marks for cadets with 'A' certificate.
  • Must have 75% attendance.
  • Break must not exceed 18 months.
  • If exceeded:

    "If on rolls for 2 years and 75% attendance, then eligible."

  • Must have passed 'B' Certificate.
  • Must be in the 2nd or 3rd year of training.
  • Must attend 75% of 3rd year syllabus.
  • Break not exceeding 18 months.
  • If exceeded:

    "On rolls for 2 years and 75% attendance, additional training required."

  • Must have attended two Camps.

Additional Information

Permission after Discharge

Cadets who left NCC may appear for exams within 12 months at their own expense if they were otherwise eligible.

Re-Test for 'C' Certificate

  • Cadets with 'C' grade in 'C' Certificate can re-test once.
  • Must reappear for the full exam.
