Terms & Conditions

Please read the following terms carefully before continuing.

1. Eligibility

All students enrolled in GMIT are eligible to join the NCC program, provided they meet physical and academic requirements set by the NCC unit.

2. Attendance

Cadets must maintain 75% attendance in all NCC training sessions, camps, and parades. Failure to do so may result in disqualification from certification.

3. Discipline

Cadets are expected to maintain high standards of discipline and follow the instructions of NCC officers and faculty coordinators at all times.

4. Certificate Criteria

Cadets who fulfill the eligibility criteria, attendance, and training standards are eligible for 'A', 'B', or 'C' certificates issued by NCC.

5. Code of Conduct

Any form of misconduct, disrespect towards faculty or fellow cadets, or violation of institute rules may lead to expulsion from the NCC unit.

6. Amendments

GMIT reserves the right to amend these terms at any time. Updates will be communicated via the official website or student communication portals.
